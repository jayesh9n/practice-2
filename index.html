Mission and Vision

Mission

To groom fully awakened and fully competent souls who will build an awakened, enlightened, physically, mentally, intellectually, spiritually, socially, economically, politically and scientifically developed family, society, nation and the world on the whole.

Objectives of Patanjali Gurukulam

Programme of Creating Vedic Scholars: This spiritual programme is based on preparing veracious scholars related to different subjects and arenas such as Grammar, Philosophy, Vedas, Upanishadas, Bhartiya Genuine History and royal duties etc., and the graduates who will be capable of showing unparallel leadership in social, spiritual, economic, scientific and political aspects.

Programme Social, Economical Scientific and Political Aspects: To prepare intellectuals and national leaders who can set new landmarks in different fields like: Technology, Management, Defence, Administrative Services, Police Services, Medicine, Engineering, Games and Sports, Fine Arts, Music, Drama, Agriculture, Industry, Research, etc.

We will be instilling in young minds the glory of both utility and attraction. Later the child will take up his way according to his/her Karmashaya, Sanskar and Swabhava. Param Pujya Swamiji believes that the possibility of reformation in a child decreases by one percent with every passing year of age. And generally from every aspect a child becomes enlightened by one percent every year. But with the help of intellectual efforts we can develop this enlightenment physically, intellectually and spiritually from one to ten percent.

Vision

Our vision at Patanjali Gurukul is to become a global centre of excellence in education that seamlessly integrates traditional values and modern knowledge. We aim to create a transformative learning ecosystem where students imbibe the best of both worlds - the depth of Vedic wisdom and the breadth of contemporary education. We envision our graduates as torchbearers of ancient wisdom and pioneers of modern progress, equipped with the skills, values, and perspectives to address the challenges of a dynamic world. With an unwavering commitment to holistic development and a deep reverence for human heritage, we aspire to cultivate leaders who shape a harmonious, prosperous, and inclusive future for humanity.
